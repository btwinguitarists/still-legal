Still Tracks — Privacy Policy

Still Tracks collects no data. None.

The stems you import are copied into the app's own storage on your device and never leave it. Playback, routing, and the sync diagnostics all run on-device. The app makes no network requests and contains no analytics, no ads, no accounts, and no third-party code that phones home.

Setlist order lives locally on your device only and disappears if you delete the app.
