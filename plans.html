Still Plans

The team side of Sunday, planned quietly. Build the order of service with a running clock — songs in their keys, readings, the message, communion. Ask people to serve and they answer with one tap; block-out dates keep you from scheduling someone who's away. One conversation per service instead of a group chat. Templates remember your usual Sunday. Setlists sync straight into Still Sets for the musicians.
