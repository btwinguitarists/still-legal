Still Sets

Worship setlists and chord charts, unhurried. Bring songs in from almost anywhere — pasted text, ChordPro, PDF, Word, Pages, OnSong backups, whole folders at once — and they come out clean: title, key, chords over the right words. Transpose, capo shapes, and Nashville numbers on every chart. Present lyrics to any screen and keep chords on stage. Works in Thai and English, together in the same song.

Support

Questions, bugs, or ideas: email pearcityguitars@gmail.com and put "Still Sets" in the subject line. I read everything.

Good to know

Your songs are plain files in your iCloud Drive — open the Files app and there they are, portable ChordPro anyone can read. Bluetooth page-turner pedals work out of the box and every button is remappable. The browser screen runs entirely on your local network: open the shown address on the projection computer and it follows your phone. Planning Center connects both ways with your own church token, only when you ask.
