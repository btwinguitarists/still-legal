Still Sets — Privacy

Still Sets collects no data. No analytics, no accounts, no tracking, no ads. Ever.

Your songs and setlists are stored on your device and, if iCloud is on, in your own iCloud Drive — visible in the Files app, under your Apple ID, never touched by us.

If you choose to connect Planning Center, the app talks directly to planningcenteronline.com with a token you create in your own church account; the token stays in your device's Keychain and nothing passes through any server of ours. Disconnect any time.

The browser-screen feature serves slides only to devices on your own local network, from your device. Nothing leaves the room.
