Still Privacy Policy

Effective July 15, 2026

Still collects nothing. There are no accounts, no analytics, no ads, and no third-party SDKs.

Microphone

Still listens to your instrument through the microphone to show its pitch. Audio is processed entirely on your device, in the moment, and is never recorded, stored, transmitted, or shared. The microphone is used only while the app is open in front of you.

Network

Still makes no network requests at all. It works exactly the same in airplane mode.
