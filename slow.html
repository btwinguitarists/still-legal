Still Slow

Learn any song by ear. Bring in a recording, loop the hard part, slow it to half speed without changing the pitch — or push past 100% to overspeed-train. Shift pitch to match a capo or an old detuned record. A trainer that creeps the loop back up 2% at a time, saved loops per song, and an export that renders exactly what you hear. The watch app is a remote, so your hands stay on the instrument.
