Still Ear

Ear training without the arcade. Hear an interval, a chord, or a four-chord progression; name it; see the truth gently and move on. Intervals anchor to songs you already know, and progressions are answered in Nashville numbers — the way musicians actually talk. Three levels per skill, freely pickable, and a review mode that quietly leans toward what you miss. The watch app stands alone: drills on your wrist, haptics carrying the verdict.

Good to know

No clock, no streaks, no shame — accuracy describes, never scolds. Tones are synthesized on the device; nothing needs the internet. Levels are suggestions, never locks.
