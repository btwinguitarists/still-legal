Still Tracks

Plays the stems you already own. A song is a folder — click, cue, pads, bass, whatever your team runs — and it plays back sample-locked, with a fader and mute per stem. One tap splits click + cue hard left and tracks hard right, so a $10 Y-cable becomes a rig. Phone calls pause the set and it resumes when iOS hands audio back.
