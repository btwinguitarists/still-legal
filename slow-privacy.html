Still Slow — Privacy Policy

Still Slow collects no data. None.

The audio files you import are copied into the app's own storage on your device and never leave it. All processing — slowing, pitch shifting, exporting — happens on-device. The app makes no network requests, contains no analytics, no ads, no accounts, and no third-party code that phones home.

Practice state (loops, speed, pitch per song) is stored locally on your device only, and disappears if you delete the app.

Exports are rendered to files that you choose where to share; sharing is handled entirely by iOS.
