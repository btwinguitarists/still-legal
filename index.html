Still

A quiet guitar tuner for iPhone and Apple Watch. The Watch app is fully standalone — microphone, pitch detection, and haptic guidance all on your wrist, no phone needed. When the string is true, everything goes still.

Support

Questions, bugs, or ideas: email pearcityguitars@gmail.com and put "Still" in the subject line. I read everything.

Good to know

Ten tunings (Standard, Drop D, DADGAD, open tunings, and more), adjustable A₄ reference, and haptic tuning on the wrist: quick ticks as you close in, a gentle tap of confirmation when you land, silence while it holds. Audio never leaves your device.
