Still IEM

Your in-ears, your mix. Plug a class-compliant audio interface into your iPhone or iPad and every input becomes a channel strip — fader, pan, three-band EQ with a high-pass, mute, solo, and a meter. Your personal mix comes out the interface's headphone jack a few milliseconds later, and the real number is measured live on screen. Host mode broadcasts every channel to your band over local WiFi; each musician joins from their own phone and mixes their own ears.

Good to know

Wired ears are the fast path — Bluetooth earbuds add a large, unavoidable radio delay, so the app labels that route "listen mode" honestly. Band mode stays entirely on your local network. Pay once. No account, no ads, no tracking — see the privacy policy.
