Still IEM — Privacy Policy

Still IEM collects no data. None.

Live audio is processed on your device in real time and is never recorded or stored. In band mode, audio streams only to the devices that join your session on your local WiFi network — it never touches the internet, and nothing is retained after the session ends.

Microphone access exists solely to monitor your audio interface's inputs (iOS treats interface inputs as the microphone). Mix settings live locally on your device only.

No analytics, no ads, no accounts, no third-party code that phones home.
